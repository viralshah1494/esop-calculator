Welcome to ESOP Calculator - your trusted tool for calculating Employee Stock Options with Indian tax rules.


Our mission is to help employees understand the true value of their stock options by providing accurate calculations that account for:

  • Perquisite Tax at Exercise
  • Short-Term Capital Gains
  • Long-Term Capital Gains
  • Tax Refunds via ITR

This calculator is designed specifically for India-based employees working at companies with ESOPs.
